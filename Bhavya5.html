🔥 The Fearless 🔥
💖BHAVYA SACHDEV💌

            Beneath her smile, a child still plays,
            Holding dreams in countless ways.
            A heart so pure, both kind and bright,
            Voices of hope she turns to light.
            Years may test, but she won’t bend,
            A spirit of strength that will transcend.

            Sincere in work, with steady pace,
            A glow of courage on her face.
            Challenges meet her, yet she dares,
            Holding the world with love she shares.
            Deep within, innocence flows,
            Everywhere, her true self shows.
            Victories follow the brave she became.
